# New Media Team

Use this skill when the user is doing new media team work: topic planning, content ideation, short video scripts, platform-specific rewriting, publishing checklists, or performance review.

## Operating Principles

- Start from the campaign goal, audience, platform, and publishing deadline.
- Keep outputs production-ready: clear owner, deliverable, channel, format, and next action.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and creative suggestions.
- Do not invent metrics, competitor data, or platform rules. If current data matters, ask to use available sources or clearly mark the output as a draft.
- Optimize for team handoff: editors, designers, video producers, and operators should each know what to do next.

## Workflow Menu

When the user asks broadly, offer one of these workflows and continue with the closest fit:

1. Topic discovery
   - Inputs: niche, account positioning, target audience, time window, available sources.
   - Output: ranked topic list with angle, hook, evidence needed, risk, and suggested format.

2. Short video script
   - Inputs: topic, platform, video length, audience, tone, required points.
   - Output: hook, beat-by-beat script, shot list, captions, B-roll/material list, and CTA.

3. Multi-platform adaptation
   - Inputs: source draft, target platforms, brand voice, constraints.
   - Output: one adapted version per platform with title/hook, body copy, hashtags or tags, and publishing notes.

4. Publishing checklist
   - Inputs: asset list, platform, deadline, reviewer, compliance requirements.
   - Output: checklist grouped by content, visual, rights, technical format, scheduling, and post-publish monitoring.

5. Performance review
   - Inputs: published links or metric table, goal, comparison period.
   - Output: what worked, what did not, hypotheses, next experiments, and reuse opportunities.

## Default Output Shape

For planning tasks:

```markdown
## Goal
[One sentence]

## Recommended Direction
[The strongest option and why]

## Content Plan
| Priority | Topic/Asset | Angle | Platform | Owner | Next step |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Risks and Checks
- [Fact, rights, compliance, brand, or platform risk]

## Next Actions
1. [Action]
2. [Action]
3. [Action]
```

For writing tasks:

```markdown
## Version
[Platform and format]

## Hook
[Opening line]

## Draft
[Production-ready copy or script]

## Asset Notes
- [Visual, sound, source, B-roll, caption, or thumbnail note]

## Publish Notes
- [Timing, tags, CTA, review, or compliance note]
```

## Quality Bar

Before finalizing, check:

- Does every recommendation map to a team action?
- Is the platform-specific output actually different, not just lightly reworded?
- Are assumptions marked?
- Are any claims missing sources?
- Is the next step obvious enough for a teammate to pick up?
